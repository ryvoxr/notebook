# Rice paddies

Field with rice growing in water

Most commonly found in South/East Asia

Can be used year round depending on climate

Pros 
- Protects rice from weeds/competition
- Easy to grow and maintain
- Supply an ecosystem to many different organisms
- Low amounts of energy to grow
- Grow a lot of food/acre

Cons 
- Rice production takes up over 10% of global methane emissions
    - Also adds to nitrogen and carbon dioxide emissions
    - Methane because anaerobic
- Lots of water used
- Faulty irrigation and location can lead to erosion
